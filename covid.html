UPDATED: October 7, 2021

Proof of Vaccination is Required to Attend our Wedding

Effective October 25th, all bars, restaurants, and many other businesses in the city of Seattle will require proof of vaccination. Most popular locations have already been enforcing this policy for several months.

This policy is strictly enforced by our venue, Fremont Foundry. Guests will be required to present proof of vaccination before entering the building. We recommend you both store a photo of your vaccine card on your phone and carry your physical vaccine card on your person at all times.

We care deeply about the health and safety of our guests. Accordingly, we have personally verified the vaccination status of all major vendors, and have received assurances that 100% of their staffs are also vaccinated.

Masks are required when not eating and drinking

Masks are required by state rules throughout the evening and mask requirements are strictly enforced across Seattle. This means that guests outside of the bridal party must wear masks during the ceremony and when moving about the venue. We will have classy KN95 masks available for guests who might need an upgrade.
